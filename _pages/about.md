## Biography

Hi there! 👋 Welcome to my personal homepage. 

I am **Sichen Xia**, a medical student currently affiliated with the **Stomatological College of Nanjing Medical University** in Nanjing, China. 

My research and clinical interests primarily focus on **Oral Epidemiology**, where I explore the intersection of population health and clinical stomatology. I am passionate about using data-driven insights to improve oral health outcomes.
